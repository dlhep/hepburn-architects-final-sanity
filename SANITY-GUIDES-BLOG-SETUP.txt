SANITY GUIDES AND BLOG SETUP

After uploading this update and Vercel shows Ready:

1. Open https://www.hepburnarchitects.net/studio
2. You will see a new section named "Guides & Blog Posts".
3. Click Create new.
4. Choose Content type: Guide or Blog post.
5. Complete the title and click Generate beside Page address.
6. Add a short summary of 80-220 characters.
7. Choose a category and publication date.
8. Add the article content using Heading 2, Heading 3, paragraphs, lists, links and images.
9. Add a featured image and image description.
10. Add an SEO title and meta description, or leave them blank to use the article title and summary.
11. Click Publish.

Published guides appear at /guides and blog posts appear at /blog.
